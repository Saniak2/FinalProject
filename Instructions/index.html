Instructions

This game is similar to wordle, but the song lengths will vary. This game is based on BLACKPINK'S songs. You will get 5 tries to guess the song. You can click on "hint" to get an image hint based on the song. Once you are done with the game, a link to the video will be given. Click on the letters and it will display on the board. Spaces are also an option as a letter. Click backspace if you want to clear a letter. Click enter when you want to submit the song. The song will only submit if you have filled in all the tiles. A white tile means that the letter is incorrect. A black tile with pink lettering means the letter is contained in the word, but in the wrong space. It also can mean that you put too much of a certain letter. A pink tile with black lettering means the letter is in the right place.
